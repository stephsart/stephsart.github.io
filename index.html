About the Work

Art, especially painting, has been a therapeutic medium I've been leveraging since my teenage years. It helps me express and work through my emotions. I don't plan most of my artwork. I go with the flow and often end up in unexpected places.

I paint two styles. One is very colorful with different shapes and forms, very dynamic and abstract. The other is more straightforward, usually a black background with a human organ painted in a vibrant color. The organ is only painted with lines and resembles biological anatomy.

One of my most truest paintings is 'Lungs filled with Blue Smoke'. This piece reflects my journey from being a heavy smoker to quitting cigarettes.
